WELCOME TO ADURCUP!

Adurcup is working to make offline advertising accountable, by providing procurement solution for F & B
establishments which help in gain relevant data points about them, enabling it create a platform for advertisers to
handpick their target customers. It enables restaurants to generate a revenue channel through advertising, who are
paid only when they report advertisement. Revenue generated through ad booking is the major driver.
